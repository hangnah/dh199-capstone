About the Project

This video highlights the struggles women workers have faced - from living lives in poverty, to having to choose between food and healthcare, and dealing with a lack of respect from their employers - all while devotedly pouring years of their lives into their work. It shows how through unity and persistence, women laborers rise up to fight for equality, fairness, and their place in the world.
